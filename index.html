About Us

Welcome to GREEN LEAF FARM HOUSE!

Nestled in the heart of Amruthur Hobli, Kunigal Taluq, Hanumapura post, Markonahalli, Karnataka, GREEN LEAF stands as a beacon of prestige and exceptional guest experiences. Situated just 3 kilometers from the scenic Markonhalli Dam, this farmhouse is a haven where nature's beauty seamlessly blends with impeccable hospitality. Enveloped by the harmonious chorus of birdsong, the air at GREEN LEAF is laced with the sweet fragrance of wildflowers and the earthy aroma of lush greenery, creating a tranquil ambiance that beckons you to embrace the serenity.

Our Commitment to Excellence

We focus on providing an unforgettable experience with personalized services and luxurious accommodations.

Registered by Department of Tourism, Government Of Karnataka.
